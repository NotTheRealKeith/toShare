Who is Bread Builder?

At Bread Builder we aim to create an application that will help young people flourish financially. As two working students ourselves, we understand the pain of juggling transport, food, rent and other costs. Through our application students and youth alike will be able to set out a simple view of all the expenses and income they have.
- Creators Lara and Keith
